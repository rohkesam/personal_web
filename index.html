Undergraduate Research

Atmospheric Rivers(AR) are large airways of moisture that direct extreme weather patterns around the globe. Through the research project, we aim to identify the best parameters and algorithms that can model ARs. This is done by using python to preform reanalysis calculations on multidimensional datasets that map AR activity over several regions.

CyberGenerations Program

CyberGenerations is a program dedicated to educating the elderly population about the basics of cybersecurity and how to safely navigate the internet. I established this program at the Westland Community Center, and over a two month period I coached Seniors through the course content. These workshops were considered highly resourceful by the Director and Seniors, and are continuing to be run by students at the University of Detroit Mercy.

Forever Young

I established this club in my highschool to bridge the gap between the younger and older generations. As a club, we visited local senior care facilities and interacted with them with games, stories, and other activities. Our goal was to enrich happiness in each senior we visited because along with their chronic diseases many of them feel isolated from society.
